In CSS3, you'll explore the power of styling web pages by controlling their appearance, layout, and behavior. CSS3 introduces advanced features like gradients, animations, and transitions, allowing you to create visually appealing and interactive designs. Understanding CSS3 empowers you to customize elements and enhance the overall user experience of websites.
